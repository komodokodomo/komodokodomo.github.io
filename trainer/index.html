WTF-Snap

A fast, easy way to craft your own interactive teaching environment - no coding expertise required!

What can I use it for?

Out of classroom learning experiences

Trails / excursions that people can go on their own

Prepare a outdoor language class

Augmented spaces

Transform your school garden into an interactive bio space

How do I use it?

1. Gather

Gather your examples into categories that you want the computer to identify.

2. Train

Train your model, then instantly test it out to see whether it can correctly classify new examples.

3. Embed

Start embedding your content to the trained categories,you can also invite others to do it to provide different perspectives and contents to the same environment. Once done, save it into the cloud for future lesson deployment, just like Kahoot!
